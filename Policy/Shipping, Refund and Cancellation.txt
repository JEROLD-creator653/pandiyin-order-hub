Shipping, Refund and Cancellation Policy
Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Thank you for shopping with Pandiyin Nature In Pack. This Shipping, Refund and Cancellation Policy explains how we process orders, shipping timelines, refund processing, and cancellation rules.

By placing an order on our website Pandiyin-Nature-in-Pack.web.app, you agree to the terms mentioned below.

1. Order Processing Time
All orders are processed within 1 to 2 business days after successful payment confirmation.

Orders may take longer during peak seasons, festivals, or due to unexpected circumstances.

2. Shipping Locations
We currently ship to:
- Tamil Nadu
- All India

International shipping is also available. International delivery charges and timelines will depend on the destination country and courier service availability.

3. Shipping Charges
A) Free Shipping
We offer free shipping for orders above ₹799.

B) Shipping Charges for Orders Below ₹799
Shipping charges apply for orders below ₹799 as follows:
- Within Tamil Nadu: ₹40
- Outside Tamil Nadu (All India): ₹80
- International Orders: Shipping charges depend on the destination country and courier partner

Final shipping charges will be displayed at checkout before payment.

4. Delivery Timeline
Estimated delivery time after shipping:
- Tamil Nadu and All India: 3 to 7 business days

Delivery times are estimated and may vary due to:
- Courier delays
- Weather conditions
- Holidays
- Remote delivery locations

5. Order Tracking
Once your order is shipped, tracking details will be shared through available communication methods such as email, WhatsApp, or SMS (based on the information provided by the customer).

6. Cancellation Policy
Orders can be cancelled only before shipping.

To cancel an order, customers must contact us as soon as possible through:
- Email
- WhatsApp
- Instagram

Once the order is shipped, cancellation is not possible.

7. Refund Policy
Refunds are applicable only if:
- The product is damaged
- The product is defective
- Wrong product is delivered
- Replacement is not possible or approved

Refunds are not provided for:
- Change of mind
- Customer order mistakes
- Opened or used products

If approved, refunds will be processed to the original payment method within 7 to 14 business days.

8. Replacement Policy
If the customer is eligible, replacement will be provided first.

Replacement requests will be approved only after verification, and unboxing video proof may be required.

9. Failed Delivery or Incorrect Address
If delivery fails due to incorrect address or incomplete contact details provided by the customer, re-shipping charges may apply.

Customers are responsible for providing correct delivery information during checkout.
