Return & Refund Policy
Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Thank you for shopping with Pandiyin Nature In Pack. We value customer satisfaction and ensure quality in all our homemade and natural products.

Please read this Return & Refund Policy carefully before placing an order.

1. Return Eligibility
Returns are accepted only in the following cases:
- Product received is damaged
- Product received is defective
- Wrong product delivered

Returns are not accepted for:
- Change of mind
- Taste preference or personal preference
- Incorrect product selected by the customer
- Opened or used products

2. Return Time Period
Customers must raise a return request within 1 to 3 days from the date of delivery.

Requests raised after this period will not be eligible for return or replacement.

3. Unboxing Video Requirement
To avoid misuse and to confirm product issues, an unboxing video is mandatory.

The unboxing video must:
- Be recorded while opening the parcel
- Clearly show the package condition and the issue
- Be submitted within 1 day of receiving the parcel

Without a proper unboxing video, return or replacement requests may be rejected.

4. Replacement Policy
If your return request is approved, we will provide a replacement first whenever possible.

Replacement will be processed after verification of the issue.

5. Refund Policy
Refunds are provided only if:
- Replacement is not possible, or
- The issue is verified and approved by our team

If approved, the refund will be processed to the original payment method within 7 to 14 business days.

6. Return Shipping Charges
- If the issue is due to our mistake (wrong product, damaged product, defective product), return shipping charges will be covered by Pandiyin Nature In Pack.
- If the return is due to customer error or incorrect order selection, return shipping charges will be paid by the customer.

7. How to Request a Return or Refund
To request a return, replacement, or refund, contact us with:
- Order ID
- Product name
- Issue description
- Unboxing video or clear photos
